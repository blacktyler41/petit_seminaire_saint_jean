    About Us

    Who we are and what we stand for

    Our school is a centre of academic excellence and character formation. We are committed to nurturing students who think critically, act responsibly, and contribute meaningfully to society.

    With dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and a peaceful learning environment, we prepare learners to excel in national exams and develop lifelong values.

    Our Patron Saint

    Saint John, is a symbol of courage, purity,and unwavering faith. He inspires our students to grow in integrity, compassion, and resilience.

    His life teaches us that greatness by the strength of character and the courage to stand for what is right.
